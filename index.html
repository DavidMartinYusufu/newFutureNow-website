Who We Are

FutureNow Development Initiative regitered with Bauchi State ministry of youth and sport, is a youth led, youth focused organisation that aims at creatin a vibrant platform for human development. Through social inclusion, civic engagement, enterprenual development, basic educatin and activism on human right

Established August 2021

Mission

To build human capacity for global action advancing policy, transforming learning and training environment, building capacities of Educators and trainers, empowering and mobilizing youth and accelerating sustainable solutions

Organisation Objective

Providing safe space for human development.

Strengthening the capacity of the communities to advocates.

Strengthening the capacityofcommunities to cater and improve children well being, by increasing access to education and livelihood(Adequate food, healthcare).

Community sensitization and mobilization on harmful practices, stigma, discrimination through creative presentation such as drama debates, awareness programs, radio engagement etc.

Increasing participation on civic engagement.

Promoting and encouraging education to families in communities.
